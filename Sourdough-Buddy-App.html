Sourdough Buddy

Your simple guide to baking sourdough bread!

Step 1: Mix Ingredients
125g starter, 325g water, 500g flour, 10g salt.
Mix starter and water first, then add flour and salt. Rest 1 hour.
Start Timer (1 hr)
Step 2: Stretch and Fold
Perform 4 sets of stretch-and-folds, 30 minutes apart.
Start Timer (2 hrs)
Step 3: Bulk Fermentation
Let dough sit covered on the counter for 3-8 hours.
Start Timer (3-8 hrs)
Step 4: Shape Dough
Shape the dough and place it in a floured banneton.
Start Timer (1 hr)
Step 5: Cold Proof (Optional)
Place in fridge covered for up to 3 days for more sour flavor.
Start Timer (Up to 3 days)
Step 6: Bake
Preheat oven to 475°F. Score and bake in a Dutch oven at 475°F for 30 minutes cov-ered, then 20 minutes uncovered at 425°F.
Start Timer (50 min)
